Contract & Execution Models

Exploring different contract and execution models to connect project owners with the most suitable delivery and collaboration approaches.

Engineering Procurement Construction

EPC (Engineering, Procurement, Construction) A turnkey model where the contractor handles design, procurement, and construction, delivering a ready-to-use asset on time and on budget.

View Model
Engineering Procurement Construction & Commissioning

A comprehensive model where the contractor manages design, procurement, construction, and commissioning, delivering a fully operational facility ready for use.

View Model
Engineering Procurement Construction & Financing

EPCF is an extended EPC model where the contractor handles design, procurement, construction, and arranges financing, combining execution with financial structuring for project sponsors.

View Model
Engineering Procurement Construction Management

EPCM is a project management-focused model where the contractor oversees engineering, procurement, and construction on behalf of the owner, ensuring the project meets schedule, budget, and quality standards without delivering a turnkey asset.

Project Finance Models

Project financing based on various financial models, designed to showcase suitable investment opportunities for investors and projects.

View All Model
Project Finance Non-Recourse / Limited Recourse

Project Finance is a financing structure based on the project's cash flows and assets. Non-Recourse limits lender claims to project assets, while Limited Recourse allows limited sponsor obligations.

View model
Corporate Finance Balance Sheet Financing

Corporate Finance, or Balance Sheet Financing, is a financing approach where the company's own balance sheet is used to fund projects. The company assumes full responsibility for debt repayment and financial risks, without isolating the project in a separate SPV.

Watch Now
Blended Finance Development & Private-Public Investment

Blended Finance combines public funds, development aid, and private investment to finance projects that are socially or environmentally impactful, leveraging subsidies, guarantees, and concessional capital to attract commercial investment.

Watch Now
Private Equity & Infrastructure Funds Long-Term Investment

Private Equity & Infrastructure Funds are pooled investment vehicles that invest in companies or infrastructure projects with the goal of long-term capital appreciation. Investors gain exposure to equity ownership, active management, and strategic influence over the assets

Watch Now

Investment & Legal Structures

Overview of investment models and legal structures designed to guide investors and project owners in planning and executing projects efficiently.

PublicPrivate Partnership PPP / P3

PublicPrivate Partnerships (PPP or P3) are cooperative arrangements between government entities and private sector partners to finance, design, build, and operate infrastructure or public service projects.

View Model
BuildTransferConcession BTC

BTC is a hybrid project model where the private sector constructs a public asset, transfers ownership to the government upon completion, and operates and maintains the asset under a concession agreement for a defined period.

View Model
Equity Investment Project Financing & Ownership

Equity investment involves providing capital in exchange for ownership shares in a project or company. Investors participate in profits, losses, and decision-making according to their equity stake.

View Model
Special Purpose Vehicle SPV

A Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) is a separate legal entity created for a specific project. It isolates project assets and liabilities from the parent company, facilitating risk management, financing, and governance.

View Model
