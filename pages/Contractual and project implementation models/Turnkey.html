Lump Sum Turnkey

Model Definition

The **Turnkey / LSTK (Lump Sum Turnkey)** model is a **full project delivery approach** where the contractor is responsible for engineering, procurement, construction, and commissioning. The contractor delivers a fully operational asset at a **fixed price**, minimizing cost uncertainty for the owner while ensuring operational readiness.

Ownership & Control

  • Ownership remains with the project sponsor
  • Contractor accountable for delivering fully operational facility
  • All execution risk (cost, schedule, quality) primarily on contractor

Strategic Applications

Greenfield Industrial Projects

LSTK contracts are ideal for projects where owners require a **fully delivered and operational asset** at a fixed price, including design, procurement, construction, and commissioning.

Infrastructure & Utilities

Applicable to roads, bridges, pipelines, and power/water plants where **fixed-price turnkey delivery** reduces cost uncertainty and ensures schedule adherence.

Project Suitability Analysis

Turnkey / LSTK is suitable for projects with **well-defined scope**, high technical complexity, and need for **guaranteed completion and operational readiness** at handover.

Risk Considerations & Mitigation

Key risks include design errors, procurement delays, construction defects, and schedule slippage. Mitigation relies on **fixed-price contracts, milestone payments, quality assurance, and performance guarantees**.
