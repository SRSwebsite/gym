Sector Overview

Construction & Urban projects cover large-scale buildings, mixed-use developments, hotels, hospitals, educational and research facilities. These projects shape urban landscapes, support economic growth, and create sustainable, high-quality infrastructure.

Ownership & Control

  • Development phases often involve EPC, design-build, or PPP contracts
